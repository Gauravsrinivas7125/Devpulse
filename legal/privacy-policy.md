# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-04-10  
**Last updated:** 2026-04-11

## 1. Introduction

DevPulse ("Service", "we", "us") is an API security scanning and LLM cost intelligence platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

- **Account information**: email address, name, company name, and authentication credentials.
- **Workspace metadata**: workspace names, team member roles, onboarding state.
- **API collection data**: imported Postman, Bruno, or OpenAPI collection files uploaded by users.
- **Scan results**: security findings, risk scores, compliance reports, and audit logs generated by the Service.
- **Billing records**: subscription tier, payment history, and invoice metadata. Full payment card details are processed exclusively by Stripe and are never stored by DevPulse.
- **Usage telemetry**: LLM token consumption, feature usage counts, and operational metrics used to improve the Service.

Users remain responsible for ensuring that the API materials they upload are lawfully shared with DevPulse.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
- Process payments and manage subscriptions.
- Enforce plan limits and feature entitlements.
- Generate security and compliance reports.
- Send transactional notifications (scan results, billing alerts).
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

## 4. Subprocessors and Third Parties

DevPulse uses subprocessors and infrastructure providers for hosting, billing, authentication, alerts, and model-based analysis where configured. Payment information is processed by Stripe and is not stored in full by DevPulse. Team administrators are responsible for managing access within their organizations.

## 5. Data Retention

DevPulse retains customer content only for as long as necessary to provide the Service, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Customers may request deletion in accordance with the service terms and applicable law.

## 6. Security

DevPulse applies reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encrypted transport (TLS), hashed credentials, webhook signature verification, and role-based access controls. No online service can guarantee absolute security. Customers should avoid uploading secrets or regulated data unless appropriate controls, contracts, and data handling terms are in place.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact the DevPulse operator identified in your commercial agreement or deployment documentation.

## 8. Contact

For privacy inquiries, data deletion requests, or compliance questions, customers should contact the DevPulse operator identified in their commercial agreement or deployment documentation.
